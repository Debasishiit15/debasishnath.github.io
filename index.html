Welcome

Dr. Debasish Nath received his Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. Currently, he is working as an Assistant Professor in School of Bioengineering and Biosciences, D Y Patil International University, Pune. His research is dedicated to the development of innovative assistive technologies that enhance the quality of life for individuals with physical impairments, particularly in the field of post-stroke rehabilitation. His primary areas of interest includes emerging technologies in healthcare e.g., Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR), Robotics, and Non-invasive Brain Stimulation techniques in neurorehabilitation, Bio-instrumentation and Medical Signal & Image Processing.
